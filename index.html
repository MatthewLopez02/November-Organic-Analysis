Daily Acquisition Trends

Daily Pattern Analysis

Organic acquisition spiked dramatically starting November 17th, with November 19th being the peak day at 1,978 users. The period from Nov 17-24 averaged 885 users/day, compared to just 213 users/day during Nov 1-16. This 4x increase suggests a significant change in organic visibility or a successful campaign driving organic traffic.

Geographic Distribution

Key Insight

New York dominates organic acquisition with 24.87% of all users (2,759), with Brooklyn alone accounting for 1,324 users (12% of total). The top 10 states represent 67.3% of all organic users, indicating strong geographic concentration in the Northeast and major metro areas.

Feature Adoption Insight

Visa Money Transfer dominates with 12,862 transactions across 2,350 users (71.3% of active users). This indicates strong remittance use case adoption among organic users. Card Purchase is second with 9,615 transactions, showing healthy everyday spending usage. The high 7-day activation rate (91.9%) suggests that users who will transact do so quickly after account creation.

Activation Analysis

Activation Overview

70.11% of November organic users have not completed any transactions yet. While this represents 7,727 inactive users, the 29.89% activation rate shows strong engagement, and these active users are showing strong transaction engagement with an average of 10.5 transactions per active user.

Key Recommendations

Strategic Actions

  • Investigate Week 3 Spike: The 4x increase in organic acquisition starting Nov 17th warrants investigation to understand what drove this growth and how to sustain it.
  • Target Week 4 Cohort: With 80.25% still inactive, these 1,946 users are still in the critical activation window. Deploy re-engagement campaigns immediately.
  • Remittance-First Onboarding: Since Visa Money Transfer is the #1 transaction type (71.3% of active users), emphasize remittance features in the onboarding flow.
  • 7-Day Window Focus: 91.9% of users who activate do so within 7 days. Concentrate all re-engagement efforts within this window.
  • Re-engage 7,727 Inactive Users: Deploy targeted push notifications and email campaigns to the inactive cohort with incentives for first transaction.
  • New York Focus: With 25% of organic users from New York (especially Brooklyn), consider geo-targeted campaigns and local partnerships in the NYC metro area.
  • Capitalize on Volume: The consistent ~32% activation rate suggests acquisition quality is stable. Focus on increasing volume while maintaining this rate.
