Key Recommendations

Strategic Actions

  • Immediate Onboarding Optimization: With 94.53% churn, focus on improving the first-time user experience and reducing friction in the activation flow.
  • Push Notification Campaign: Target the 1,470 users who haven't transacted with personalized reminders and incentives to complete their first transaction.
  • Week 2 Cohort Investigation: Analyze what was different about Week 2 acquisition (Nov 8-14) that resulted in near-zero activation despite having 2+ weeks to convert.
  • Android-First Strategy: Given 74.7% of organic users are on Android, prioritize Android app store optimization and Android-specific features.
  • Card Purchase Promotion: Since Card Purchase is the dominant transaction type, emphasize card activation and spending benefits in onboarding.
  • 7-Day Activation Window: 61.2% of users who activate do so within 7 days. Focus re-engagement efforts heavily on the first week post-signup.
